Request new tag

What happens when I request a new tag?

  1. The SWID group will review the proposed tag and, if needed, modify it in accordance with the national standard.
  2. Once the SWID group reviews and approves the tag, they will add it to the repository.
  3. You will be notified by email when the new tag has been added to the repository within 5-7 business days.
  4. You can then visit the repository to retrieve and apply the new tag.
