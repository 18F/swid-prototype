Request new tag

What happens whens I request a new tag?

  1. Click “submit” to send the new tag request to the government SWID group.
  2. The SWID group will review the proposed tag and, if needed, modify it in accordance with the national standard (link to NIST).
  3. Once the SWID group reviews and approves the tag, they will add it to the repository. The review and approval process typically takes 5-7 business days.
  4. You will be notified when the new tag has been added to the repository.
  5. You can then visit the repository to retrieve and apply the new tag.
